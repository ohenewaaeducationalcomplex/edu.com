About Our School

OHENEWAA EDUCATIONAL COMPLEX is committed to nurturing the minds and talents of young learners in a supportive and innovative environment. Established in 2012, our institution has grown to become a beacon of academic excellence and character development.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for excellence, innovation, and producing responsible global citizens.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and inspiring environment where students are encouraged to reach their full potential academically, socially, and morally.

Our Values

  • Respect
  • Excellence
  • Leadership
  • Innovation
